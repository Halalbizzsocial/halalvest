Step 1: Application

Begin by completing our simple application form. We'll need basic details about your business, including:

  • Business name and location
  • Industry and business type
  • Current stage (startup, growth, mature)
  • Funding requirements and objectives
  • Brief business overview

Our team will review your application within 2-3 business days.

Step 2: Shariah Verification

Our Shariah compliance team will review your business activities to ensure they align with Islamic principles:

  • No involvement in prohibited industries (alcohol, gambling, etc.)
  • Business operations free from interest (riba)
  • Minimal debt ratio (typically below 33%)
  • Ethical business practices

We may request additional documentation during this phase.

Step 3: Complete Your Profile

After initial approval, you'll build your complete business profile:

  • Detailed business plan and financial projections
  • Team information and background
  • Market analysis and competition
  • Use of funds and expected returns
  • Supporting documents (financial statements, legal documents)
  • Photos, videos, and other media

Our team provides guidance throughout this process to create an attractive profile.

Step 4: Go Live and Connect

Once your profile is complete and approved:

  • Your business listing goes live on HalalVest
  • Matched with suitable investors based on criteria
  • Receive notifications when investors show interest
  • Communicate directly with potential investors
  • Access to our dashboard to track engagement

Our platform facilitates the connection, but final investment terms are negotiated directly between you and investors.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses can list on HalalVest?

HalalVest welcomes businesses from all sectors that operate in compliance with Shariah principles. This means your business must avoid prohibited activities like interest-based financing, gambling, alcohol, pork products, and other non-halal goods or services. Industries that typically qualify include technology, healthcare, education, manufacturing, retail, food & beverage (halal), real estate, and ethical services.

How much does it cost to list my business?

Basic listing on HalalVest is free. We charge a success fee of 3-5% only when your business successfully secures funding through our platform. This ensures our interests are aligned with yours - we only succeed when you do. Premium listing options with enhanced visibility and promotion are available for a monthly subscription fee starting at $99/month.

How long does the verification process take?

The initial review of your application typically takes 2-3 business days. The complete Shariah compliance verification can take 7-14 days to complete.
