Detailed Process

1. Create Your Account

Register with your email or social accounts. Complete your investor profile with your investment preferences, risk tolerance, and financial goals.

What you'll need:

  • Valid email address
  • Basic personal information
  • Investment preferences

2. Verification Process

Complete our KYC (Know Your Customer) verification process to ensure compliance with financial regulations and Shariah standards.

Documents required:

  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
  • Additional documentation for accredited investors

3. Explore Investment Opportunities

Browse through our curated selection of Shariah-compliant investment opportunities across various sectors and regions.
